The Vectrex was originally developed and sold by GCE (General Consumer Electronics). Milton Bradley acquired GCE in 1983 and continued selling the console under the MB brand.

ROM files sourced from [malbanGit/Vide](https://github.com/malbanGit/Vide/tree/master/system), the VIDE Vectrex emulator project. The `version` field on each entry records the 4-character community checksum identifier used by Vectrex tools to distinguish ROM revisions.
